DESK UPGRADE DURING THE QUARANTINE

  • 21th April 2020
  • iv40

In these days of quarantine and working from home, my desk was so basis that it needed some improvement. In the image below you can see my initial set up with ordinary monitor/tv "Toshiba" (yeah I know there are some big bezels :) ). The bad part is that because it's an old school tv it doesn't have any type of protection and my eyes were geting tired very fast.


Working with one monitor as a web developer is not so easy, even as a junior web developer. So I decided to search online for something reasonable and not so expensive, but at the same time with modern design so I ended up with AOC 21B1XHS for around 90€.

The next thing I needed was monitor stands because I don't have much space on the desk to place them side by side and also need to place the monitor in center a little bit higher so a speakers can be placed below it (saving space). For the stands I choose ARCTIC Z1 Basic - Desk Mount Single Monitor Arm they seems very stable and although they are cheap - around 30€.

Great! Monitors and stands are all set.

I had a little bit extra money so I decided to purhcase one good keyboard, a good mouse and speakers. For these I went for Logitech G213 - keyboard (~65€), Logitech G502 Hero - for the mouse (~75€) and Logitech Z333 for the speakers (~40€).

The cable management needs improvement but overall the new desk set up looks and feel very good. (The chair is my old one).
